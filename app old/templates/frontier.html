How this works

Each dot represents a randomly generated portfolio using your holdings, with weights that sum to 100%. The frontier is the upper-left edge of the cloud — portfolios that maximise return for their level of risk. Any portfolio below-right of this curve is suboptimal. Max Sharpe (★) maximises return per unit of risk. Min Volatility (◆) minimises total risk. Your portfolio (●) shows where you currently sit relative to these theoretical optima. <%= synthetic_note %> If showing simulated data, upload a historical price CSV in Settings for real correlations.
