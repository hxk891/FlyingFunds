Our story

Where it came from

The problem we saw

Most investment apps fall into two camps: oversimplified (great for beginners, useless for learning) or overwhelming (powerful but impenetrable without a finance degree).

Meanwhile, students and self-taught investors are left trying to connect theory to practice with spreadsheets and disconnected tools. There is no single platform that tracked your portfolio AND taught you what the numbers meant.

What we built

FlyingFunds started as a final-year University project at the intersection of software engineering and investment management. The goal: a platform that doesn't just show you your Sharpe ratio, but explains why it matters, how to improve it, and what the theory says.

The result is a platform that tracks real portfolios with live data, teaches finance through interactive tools, and grows with you from your first stock to a diversified multi-asset portfolio.
